Our Legal Services

At Advocate K.H. Mantsha, we offer a comprehensive range of legal services to meet your needs. Our experienced team provides expert guidance and representation in the following areas:

Civil Litigation

Expert representation in civil disputes, ensuring your rights are protected throughout the legal process.

Deceased Estates

Compassionate and professional handling of estate administration and inheritance matters.

Divorce Matters

Sensitive guidance through divorce proceedings, focusing on fair settlements and minimal stress.

Drafting of Wills

Ensure your assets are distributed according to your wishes with professionally drafted wills.

Debt Collection

Effective strategies to recover outstanding debts while maintaining professional relationships.

Road Accident Fund

Assistance with RAF claims to secure compensation for injuries sustained in road accidents.

Contracts

Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating contracts to protect your interests and ensure legal compliance.

Expert guidance in claiming benefits from pension and provident funds.

Assistance with workplace injury claims to ensure fair compensation.

Unlawful Arrest & Detention Claims

Protecting your rights and seeking justice for unlawful arrest or detention.
